Credit Card Fraud Detection

"Can machine learning models detect fraudulent credit card activity efficiently, and how does a hybrid classical–quantum approach compare?"

Problem Statement

Credit card fraud causes billions in losses. Fraudulent transactions are rare and hidden among normal ones. Existing systems produce false alarms and miss complex fraud patterns.

Methodology

Dataset: ULB Credit Card Dataset

Models: Random Forest, XGBoost, Quantum VQC

Results

Model achieved 98% accuracy and high recall.
